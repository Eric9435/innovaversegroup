Nexus-WB55 Dual-Core Development Board

The Nexus-WB55 platform is engineered around the STM32WB55CEU6 dual-core architecture, integrating impedance-controlled 50Ω RF trace routing and high-speed signal integrity optimization for reliable, programmable deployment of multiprotocol wireless systems.
